Welcome!

Mr. Corica

Welcome to our web site! Members of the 2012–2013 Computer Science class at Peddie School produced the work shown below over the course of the year, along with many projects not included below.

It’s important to know that none of the students represented below had done any programming in Java before starting the course, with the majority having done no programming at all! All students began the course using Processing, spent just two trimesters working on various projects, and the third trimester preparing for the AP Computer Science test.

While the group did very little “book learnin’”, everyone encountered challenges in striving to make their ambitious projects work, and learned, in deep ways, the important ideas at the core of all great programming!

Very special thanks and admiration go to Jiehan Zheng ’14, an extraordinarily capable programmer who took on the task of creating this site to show off others’ work! His own work was deep and complex, but not (unfortunately) easy to demonstrate on a web site! We all wish him luck as he goes off to Columbia this summer to do research in computer natural language processing in Prof. Rambow’s lab.

This 2012–2013 computer science group was the most enthusiastic and accomplished group in many years, setting a new standard for Peddie programming!
